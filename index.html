Music Therapy for Pregnancy, Birth, and Beyond

Welcome! I'm so glad you are here.

I specialize in using music therapy to help parents and babies in the prenatal and postpartum period. Navigating the many changes that come with this phase of life can be exciting and challenging. Music offers a safe space for parents and babies to connect and be taken care of. I offer a variety of services, from single-session birth music consultations to ongoing music psychotherapy.
