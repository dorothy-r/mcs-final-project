Music Therapy for Pregnancy, Birth, and Beyond

About Me

I am a nationally board-certified music therapist, with an MA in Music Therapy from New York University. After finishing my degree, I completed advanced training in Vocal Psychotherapy with a focus on working with trauma survivors, and worked in mental health settings for several years. Later, inspired by the birth of my own children, I pursued training in the Sound Birthing Method of music therapy. Since then, my work has focused on pregnancy, birth, and the postpartum period. However, people in all phases of life can benefit from music therapy, and I am happy to work with anyone who is interested in exploring music as a healing modality.
